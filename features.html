    General

    Ad Block

    Make the advertisments in the lobby which block your view vanish

    Custom Server List

    Custom server list that you can use while not connected to the lobby. You can also make this one replace the lobby one

    FlagClash

    Friends

    When somebody is your friend you can't attack them. You can add friends by sneaking and pressing middle click on a player. To remove a friend just middle click the player again

    Flag Upgrade time

    You can see how long it takes to upgrade your flag by opening your flag and checking the title of the flag menu. This value is affected by multipliers but doesn't take in count the time that the multiplier lasts, so it can be little off

    Food Information

    By holding a food item in your hand, you can see the effects of it. The information is shown where the food bar should be located

    Flag Break Time

    When you break your flag you can see how long it will take for it to disappear above it measured in seconds

    Revoker Radius

    You can see the radius of placed revokers with a visual sphere

    Starite Tracers

    Yes, this is allowed by the creator. Spawns visual lines which lead you to spawning starite sources

    Item Entity Tooltip

    With this you can see tooltips of floating items. This can be used to see shop items beforehand.
